This summer, I had an opportunity to hike Section J of the Pacific Crest Trail with a group friends. I would like to use this page to share my experiences in preparation for such an endevour.

Gear & Packing Tips

Most first-time through-hikers make a grave mistake of overpacking. So did I during my section hike in Great Smoky Mountains National Park back in 2017. I remember carrying a giant backpack and wearing shoes that were a size too tight. Needless to say, the trip could have been much more enjoyable if I did my research. So while preparing for my PCT hike this summer, I came across the YouTuber Dixie aka Homemade Wanderlust. I found her videos very helpful.
